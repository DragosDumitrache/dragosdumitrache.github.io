I'm Dragos — a Staff Engineer based in Romania, originally from Bucharest, with a few years in London along the way. I studied AI at Imperial College London, and I've been building backend systems and leading engineering teams ever since.

I'm currently at **Tide**, where I previously managed two engineering teams working on European expansion. I've since moved into a purely Staff IC role, focused on future-proofing their platform.

Outside of work, I'm building **Honk** (more on that later) — a project I'm genuinely excited about, but not quite ready to talk about in detail yet.

## Why this site

I've had a CV page here for a while, but I wanted somewhere to write. Not a polished publication — mostly raw thoughts when I have something to say. Fair warning: I'm very opinionated, and these posts will reflect that. If you're looking for diplomatic takes, this probably isn't the blog for you.

Topics I'll likely ramble about:

- **Backend and infrastructure** — Python, AWS, Kubernetes, the tools I reach for daily
- **Developer experience** — making engineers productive is something I care about deeply
- **Engineering leadership** — the human side of building software, which turns out to be the hard part
- **Side projects** — and the occasional shadow project, things I'm building in the open or quietly in the dark

No schedule, no editing passes. Just writing when something feels worth sharing.
